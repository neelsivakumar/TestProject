Once upon a time...

As the mountains & skies prayed, Moon Master, along with his nemesis, ZeroKiller, arose to create an elite group of heroes.

A respected London trader by day, a fearsome beast by night, he was once unstoppable in his drive to boost small companies into big ones and create a solid financial foundation for new businesses to market and build.

During a FULL MOON he was known to create market events that led to huge tokens. He shilled with such efficiency that all coins bowed to his influence and mooned like never before. MoonMaster is a legend. MoonMaster is a HERO.

During the last Great Token Hero War, MoonMaster was forever changed, awaiting his chance to avenge his nemesis ZeroKiller.

An Ex-Special Forces operative who primarily worked in the military hardware and crypto spheres, his efficiency with prototype High Energy Laser weapons meant he became an important contributor to the global development of military laser solutions. After a distinguished military career, ZeroKiller created a consultancy and development firm specializing in the latest-tech military hardware with an emphasis on Solid State Laser rifles.

As part of his ambition to be the prime developer of laser weapons, ZeroKiller commissioned an armored bodysuit with integrated laser eye weapons which he personally wears and operates. In 2021, ZeroKiller was contracted by Token Heroes to assist them in the high-energy destruction of zeros on the Market Caps of suitable crypto coins. When the time is right, ZeroKiller uses his epic laser-eye weapons to target unwanted zeros.

In combination with an ability to mobilize whole communities to carry out shilling programs designed by the legendary MoonMaster, ZeroKiller, is a highly effective zero-killing machine. Unfortunately, his alliance with MoonMaster was destroyed during the Great Token Hero War. If you are a zero, be afraid, be VERY afraid.
